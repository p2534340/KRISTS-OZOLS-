John Wick: Chapter 3 Parabellum

The film starts with action straight away. A bounty of 14 million has been put on John Wick's head for killing a member of the international assassin's guild. The "High Table" sends brutal assassins to eliminate John Wick and strips him from any services that could help him. John Wick gets chased throughout the whole of New York as the ruthless assassins try to claim the bounty. The film contains epic fighting scenes and non-stop action throughout the entire film, exactly what you would expect from a John Wick film.
